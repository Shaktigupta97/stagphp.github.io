    Contribute in StagPHP

    Small things sometimes mean a whole lot!

    Welcome to the StagPHP contribution page. Join our community. Our community will love to have you on board. We appreciate all the efforts made to enhance this opensource framework. We value your smallest contribution because small things sometimes mean a whole lot! Your participation will accelerate the development of StagPHP. Please, consider joining the Discord channel before you start.

    Join Discord

    Become a contributor

    Join our discord channel. Connect with our community members. Let them know how can you contribute to the StagPHP project. Our community members may ask you to introduce yourself. And assign tasks based on your interests. You just need to complete the task to become a contributor.

    How can you contribute

    There are a lot of things to do around in our small community. some of them are listed below. Just do the things you love. Connect with our community members. They will assign tasks based on your interests.

    Development

    PHP developers are welcome to improve StagPHP. To do so, just fork our repository and start coding. You can create a merge request. Inform the community about the changes you have made or bug you have fixed. Once your merge request is accepted, you will become a part of our developer community.

    Introduction

    This is a simple and important contribution. Just introduce StagPHP to your friends, and tell them to follow StagPHP on twitter, or to join our discord channel. By doing so, you are helping our community to grow. Just connect with our community members to know more.

    Content and Assets

    Everything on the internet needs content and artwork. You can start contributing by writing content for the StagPHP website. Or provide us relevant images for social media channels and websites. Complete documentation, write examples, tutorials, and more. Just connect with our community members to know exactly what we need.

    Join Community

    Join our Discord channel and you will be a part of our community. Connect with our community members. They will assign tasks based on your interests. Complete the task and become a contributor. Please don't work anonymously. We don't like ghosts working in our community.
